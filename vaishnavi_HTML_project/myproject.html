  1. ARJIT SINGH

    2. Arijit Singh (born 25 April 1987) is an Indian playback singer and music composer.
    [6][4][2] The recipient of several accolades including a National Film Award and seven Filmfare Awards,
    he has recorded songs in several Indian languages, predominantly Hindi and Bengali.




  2. NEHA KAKKAR

    3. Neha Kakkar Singh is an Indian playback singer. She is the younger sister of playback singers Tony Kakkar and Sonu Kakkar.
    She began performing at a very early age at religious events.
    In 2005, she participated in the second season of the singing reality show, Indian Idol



  3. LATA MAGESHKAR

    4. Lata Mangeshkar was an Indian playback singer and occasional music composer.
    She is considered to be one of the greatest and most influential singers of the Indian subcontinent.
    Her voice was one of the unifying elements of the people of South Asia across borders.
